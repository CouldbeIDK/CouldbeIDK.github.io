What We Are Trying to do.

We at the PUC Green Energy group are trying to get a kick starter going to replace some of the light bulbs around campus, from Low-Pressure Sodium Bulbs to Light Emitting Diode Bulbs.

Why LED light bulbs?

While LED light bulbs are typically more expensive at the moment of purchase, they make up for that cost in the long term, by saving energy while still providing a brighter light than that emitted by Low-Pressure Sodium Bulbs. For more information check out the articles at the bottom of the page.

Why we need your help.

As mentioned before, the upfront costs for LED bulbs is higher by comparison, so we need your help to give us the capital we need to start this project.
